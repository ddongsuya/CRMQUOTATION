[8] 18. At least 10 animals (five female and five male) should be used at each dose level. If interim euthanasia are planned, the number should be increased by the number of animals scheduled to be euthanised before the completion of the study. Consideration should be given to an additional satellite group of ten animals (five per sex) in the control and in the top dose group for observation of reversibility, persistence, or delayed occurrence of toxic effects, for at least 14 days post treatment.
