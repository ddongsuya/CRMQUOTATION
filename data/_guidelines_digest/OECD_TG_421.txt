[11] 421 OCDE/OECD ANNEX 1 DEFINITIONS Reproduction toxicity represents harmful effects on the progeny and/or an impairment of male and female reproductive functions or capacity. Maternal toxicity: adverse effects on gravid females, occurring either specifically (direct effect) or aspecifically (indirect effect). Impairment of fertility represents disorders of male or female reproductive functions or capacity.
