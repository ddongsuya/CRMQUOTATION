[45] 13. As a laboratory gains experience and conducts more studies, the historical control databases can expand to include both individual animal data and group mean data. [46] 15. A historical negative control database (HCD) should consist of data from at least ≥ 20 independent studies and ≥ 100 individual animals. These values are consistent with recommendations by Hayashi et al. [2011] and Igl and colleagues [2019].
